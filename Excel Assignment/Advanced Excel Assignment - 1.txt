1. What do you mean by cells in an excel sheet?

ans. In an Excel, cells refer to boxes where you can enter the data. Cells are identified by column letter and row number like A1.

2. How can you restrict someone from copying a cell from your worksheet?

ans. To restrict,
     * first select the cell or worksheet,
	 * then right click on selected cell then choose 'format cell'
	 * Then goes to 'protection' tab and uncheck the 'locked' checkbox if select
     * After that goes to 'review' tab in excel ribbon
     * click on 'protect sheet'
     * Set a password and then ok	 

3. How to move or copy the worksheet into another workbook?

ans. To move or copy,
     * first goes to bottom of the sheet's tab and then right click
	 * then goes to 'move or copy',
	 * after that select the target workbook or location in drop down list
	 * before click on the 'ok' button make sure to check the 'create a copy' checkbox

6. When to use a relative cell reference in excel?

ans. Relative cell references in Excel are used when you want a formula or function to adjust its cell references automatically 
     based on the relative position of the formula.
	 example - you have a formula like A1+B1 in cell C1, then this formula automatically changes to = A2+B2 in cell C2
